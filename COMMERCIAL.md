# Commercial licensing

potocolom is licensed under the [GNU AGPL-3.0](LICENSE). That license is enough for almost everyone:

- Self-hosting for yourself, your team or your company is free, including internal commercial use.
- Modifying the code for your own use is free.
- Contributing changes back is free and welcome (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)).
- Running an unmodified copy as a service is free; the source obligation is already met by this repository.

You need a commercial license when you modify potocolom and do not want to meet the AGPL's source obligations: distributing a modified version requires conveying its source (sections 4 to 6), and offering a modified version to others over a network requires the same (section 13). If you cannot publish your changes under the AGPL, the commercial license is the alternative.

A commercial license grants an exception to the AGPL for your use, is negotiated per case, and typically includes an attribution and link-back to this project. To start the conversation, open a discussion on this repository or contact the maintainer through the address on the project website.

This document is a plain-language summary, not legal advice; the [LICENSE](LICENSE) text governs.
